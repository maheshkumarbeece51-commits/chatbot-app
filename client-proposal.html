1 The Problem We're Solving
📞

Missed Calls & Bookings

Patients call after hours or during busy periods and can't get through, leading to lost appointments.

⏰

Staff Time Wasted

Receptionists spend hours each day manually booking, confirming, and rescheduling appointments.

📋

No-Shows & Gaps

Without automated reminders, patients forget appointments — leaving costly gaps in the schedule.

🌙

Zero After-Hours Support

Patients have no way to book or get information outside of clinic hours.

2 Proposed Solution

A custom AI chatbot embedded on your clinic's website and/or WhatsApp that handles appointment booking end-to-end — from greeting the patient to confirming the slot and sending reminders.

💬

Smart Conversational Flow

Guides patients step-by-step to collect name, contact, doctor preference, and time slot.

📅

Live Calendar Integration

Connects to Google Calendar to show real-time availability and block confirmed slots.

📲

WhatsApp Confirmation

Sends instant booking confirmation and 24-hour reminder via WhatsApp or SMS.

🌐

24/7 Availability

Works around the clock — patients can book at midnight just as easily as at noon.
